Contact

Clear fit saves time.

The fastest useful conversation starts with scope, ownership, location expectations, and the real problem behind the role. I am most interested in work where the system itself can still be improved.

Best first message

  • role title and real ownership
  • core systems problem to solve
  • location and remote expectations
  • salary or rate range

Strong fit

  • AI systems architecture
  • platform engineering with tooling scope
  • workflow design and internal automation
  • hands-on architecture with real implementation authority

Poor fit

  • pure endpoint administration
  • low-agency support environments
  • architecture roles with no route to execution
  • heavy bureaucracy around obvious system fixes
